Introduction

This process book describes the steps my team and I took in designing a solution to the problem University of Washington students have of not being able to easily find events when they are outside their on-campus communities, because they lack awareness of when, where, and what events are going on around them.

The prompt was simply, "Surviving College" and we were asked to find a problem that fell into that category. This website outlines how our team arrived at our final design, as well as my specific contributions to the project.
